# Welcome to the HedgeDoc User Guide

**HedgeDoc** is a real-time, multi-platform collaborative markdown note editor. This means that you can write notes with other people on your **desktop**, **tablet** or even on the **phone**.

This guide is designed to help you get the most out of HedgeDoc, whether you are writing your first note or creating complex presentations.

## What you will find here

* [**Interface**](./interface.md): Learn about the workspace, view modes, and navigation.
* [**Managing Notes**](./managing-notes.md): How to create, save, share, and import/export your notes.
* [**Editor Features**](./editor.md): Master the editor with shortcuts, auto-complete, and specialized modes like Slide Mode.
* [**Markdown Syntax**](./formatting.md): A comprehensive reference for all supported Markdown syntax, including diagrams, math, and alerts.

Happy writing!
